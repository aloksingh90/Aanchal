how it all started

A cup of tea, and a kind of magic I'd never seen

Our first date was simple — just tea, and you telling me about your world. And then you told me about your meditation. The kind that feels almost supernatural, magical — something I'd never come across in anyone before.

I remember sitting there thinking I'd stumbled onto someone rare. I still think that. I think it more now.

all the moments

The little map of us

None of it photographed — but all of it remembered.

✦ moment one

You and your city

You told me how you showed your mom and dad around Dubai — walking them through the city you've made entirely your own. I loved how proud you were of it.

✦ moment two

Westin Cafe, and a Barsaati that wasn't open

You took me to Westin Cafe — your favourite place to eat. Then to Barsaati, which turned out to be closed. We laughed it off and the night was still ours anyway.

✦ moment three

My worst planning, our best detour

I'll own it — I had no plan. I dragged you to Bur Dubai with absolutely no idea where I was going, and somehow we ended up at Kite Beach. Terrible logistics. Perfect evening.

✦ moment four

The Marina, three years later

At the Marina — your favourite place — you stood exactly where you'd stood three years ago, and told me how you'd manifested this whole life in Dubai. And that was the moment we kissed for the first time. I'll never forget where I was standing.

✦ moment five

Shopping for me, and a film called Jackson

We went out to shop (for me), and somehow ended up watching Jackson. We were both convinced it would be boring. It turned out to be beautiful — which, honestly, is how a lot of things go with you.

✦ moment six

JBR, and the great Dubai Frame mix-up

You showed me your spot at JBR beach. And you told me about the time you confidently sent people looking for the Dubai Frame to a hotel right in front of the big wheel instead. Still one of the funniest, most you things I know.

✦ moment seven

One acai bowl, shared

We shared the acai bowl you love. Small moment. Stayed with me.

✦ moment eight

An evening at Global Village

You took me to Global Village and we had the kind of night I keep quietly replaying when I'm not paying attention.
